# Welcome
Hi and Welcome to the <b style={styles.common}>React Redux Template</b> StoryBook!<br />
Here you can find <b style={styles.common}>description of common components</b> and modules used in the creation of the system.
<br />


### Audience
This document is meant for:
- <b style={styles.common}>Devs</b><br />
      If you're <b style={styles.common}>new to the project</b>, it might be helpful to look around and get familiar with the main building blocks and their behavior.<br />
      If you're <b style={styles.common}>familiar with the project</b>, you may want to verify that your components look and feel as expected.
      <br />
      <br />

- <b style={styles.common}>QAs</b><br />
      Testing Application features always comes with the possibility that <b style={styles.common}>one component blocks the other</b> or when <b style={styles.common}>an error in one place trigger a problem elsewhere</b>.<br />
      StoryBook provides <b style={styles.common}>isolated environment</b> for all component which makes them easier to test and talk about it.<br />
      Following the <b style={styles.common}>simple steps of updating just the right properties</b> makes easier to <b style={styles.common}>reproduce results</b> and shared them between team members.
      <br />
      <br />
